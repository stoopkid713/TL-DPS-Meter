# STOOP Roadmap

STOOP is a free, open-source party DPS meter for Throne & Liberty. It reads the game's local
combat log to show a live party scoreboard and boss parses — built for **self-improvement**, not
leaderboard-shaming.

This is where we're headed. Priorities shift with your feedback — drop ideas in the Discord
`#suggestions` / `#feature-requests`.

> **How we prioritize:** a meter has to be *trustworthy* before it's fancy. We fix accuracy and
> setup friction first, then add depth, then grow. Numbers you can't trust aren't worth charting.

---

## 🔨 Now — trust & first value
*Making the meter rock-solid and easy to turn on.*

- **Reliable recording** — no more silently missed logs, dropped long fights, or "Recording…" spam.
- **Accurate party totals** — the scoreboard shows the true kill total across multi-phase bosses.
- **Effortless setup** — guided help to find and enable the in-game combat-log setting (the #1 thing new users get stuck on).
- **Steadier parties & overlay** — fewer reconnect hiccups and state desyncs.

## 📋 Next — depth & lower friction
*Removing the last adoption barriers and deepening the analysis.*

- **Signed installer** — no more "unknown publisher" warning on download.
- **One-click share** — share a parse or scoreboard with a tiny link.
- **Per-target breakdown** — see your damage split across every boss/add.
- **Deeper analytics** — a richer dashboard (boss trends, hit quality, fight depth).
- **Accuracy & crit stats** — crit / heavy / hit-quality numbers.

## 💭 Later — reach & polish
*Growth loops and platform niceties.*

- **Public leaderboards** — opt-in, by content type & tier (on trustworthy data).
- **Spectator mode** — watch a party's board without being in it.
- **Desktop polish** — custom window, system tray, start-at-boot, auto game-detect.
- **Community tools** — explainer content, support tickets, `tldps://` quick-join links.
